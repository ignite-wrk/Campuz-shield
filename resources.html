How to Create a Strong Password

  • Use at least 12 characters
  • Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid dictionary words or personal data
  • Don't reuse passwords between sites
  • Consider a trusted password manager

Tip: Use passphrases like Blue-Sky@Running-Fast-2024!

How to Spot Fake Messages

  • Check the sender's email/phone number carefully
  • Look for spelling and grammar mistakes
  • Hover over links before clicking
  • Never share passwords via email or SMS
  • Verify urgent requests by contacting the organization directly

Protecting Your Digital Footprint
